The Review

* * * * *

The Shadow of the Wind

Carlos Ruiz Zafon


A gripping tale set in the heart of Barcelona's Cemetery of Forgotten
Books. A masterpiece of atmosphere and mystery.

The Verdict

Zafon reminds us why we fell in love with reading in the first place.
The prose is lush, the characters are haunting, and the plot is perfectly
paced

Must Read: Definitely.
