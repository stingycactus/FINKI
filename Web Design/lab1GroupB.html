School subjects help students build knowledge, improve thinking, and develop practical skills for everyday life. Science teaches students how the natural world works, from the movement of objects to the structure of living organisms. Language classes help students communicate clearly, read with understanding, and express their ideas in speech and writing.

Regular learning, practice, and participation are important for success in every subject.
